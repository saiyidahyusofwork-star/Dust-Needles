ABOUT The Dust & Needles Story

Dust & Needles Archive

We are a group of music enthusiasts dedicated to curating the best vintage vibes. Dust & Needles was born out of a profound love for analog sound in a digital world. We wanted to recreate the feeling of digging through crates of old records, finding a hidden gem, and letting the needle drop.

Built with passion. Our team aims to provide a seamless, distraction-free environment to discover mood-based playlists.
