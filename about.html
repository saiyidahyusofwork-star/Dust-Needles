The Dust & Needles Story

Final Year Project (FYP) 2026


Dust & Needles was born out of a profound love for analog sound in a digital world. We wanted to recreate the feeling of digging through crates of old records, finding a hidden gem, and letting the needle drop.


Built with HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, and the Spotify Web API. Our team aims to provide a seamless, distraction-free environment to discover mood-based playlists.
